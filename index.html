About

I have more than 7 years of experience as a software engineer specializing in software design and process. My skills allow me to create efficient and scalable solutions that minimize heavy load.

I am enthusiastic about creating inventive software solutions and have expertise in developing back-end microservices, primarily using Java and springboot and framework, but I also have experience with other platforms like ServiceNow.

Throughout my career, I've tackled diverse projects such as integrating ServiceNow with other systems, implementing data backup solutions, setting up queueing systems, and load balancing traffic for a warehouse, all without relying on cloud services. However, I also have experience with cloud technologies; for instance, I utilized Azure to develop a restaurant recommendation system for a prominent hotel and casino group.

In my spare time, I find pleasure in exploring new tools and enhancing the infrastructure supporting various services. I also have a keen interest in experimenting with machine learning models such as neural networks and genetic algorithms.
